About Us

Events of Goa is a platform dedicated to showcasing the vibrant and diverse events happening across Goa. From cultural festivals, beach carnivals, food fests, music concerts, to village traditions — everything is in one place.

Our mission is to help both locals and tourists discover, explore, and enjoy the beauty and excitement of Goa’s celebrations.

We provide updated details of events happening in Panaji, Calangute, Baga, Anjuna, Margao, Vasco, and more.

Whether you're planning your next adventure or simply exploring — we ensure you never miss an event in Goa!
