About Us

Events of Goa is a platform dedicated to showcasing the vibrant and diverse events happening across Goa. From cultural festivals, beach carnivals, food fests, music concerts, to local village traditions — we bring everything to one place.

Our mission is to make it easier for everyone — locals and tourists — to discover, explore, and enjoy the beauty and excitement of Goa’s celebrations.

We provide updated details of events happening in Panaji, Calangute, Baga, Anjuna, Margao, Vasco, and other beautiful regions.

Whether you're planning your next adventure or just exploring what’s new — we make sure you never miss an event in Goa!
